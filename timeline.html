TIMELINE

Naruto's Epic Journey

Follow the path from outcast to Hokage

Childhood

The Outcast

Naruto grows up alone in the Hidden Leaf Village, shunned by the villagers who fear the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed within him. Despite the loneliness, he dreams of becoming Hokage to gain recognition.

Academy Days

Team 7 Formation

Naruto graduates from the Ninja Academy and is placed on Team 7 with Sasuke Uchiha, Sakura Haruno, and their sensei Kakashi Hatake. This marks the beginning of his true ninja journey.

First Mission

Land of Waves Arc

Team 7's first major mission takes them to the Land of Waves, where they face the demon of the mist, Zabuza, and his apprentice Haku. Naruto learns the true meaning of being a ninja.

Genin

Chunin Exams

The Chunin Exams test young ninjas' abilities. Naruto faces powerful opponents, learns new jutsu, and witnesses Sasuke's growing obsession with power after encountering Orochimaru.

Betrayal

Sasuke's Departure

Sasuke leaves the village to seek power from Orochimaru. Naruto leads a mission to bring him back, resulting in their first major battle at the Valley of the End.

Time Skip

Training with Jiraiya

Naruto spends two and a half years training with Jiraiya, one of the legendary Sannin. He returns to the village stronger and more mature, ready to face new challenges.

Shippuden

Akatsuki Threat

The criminal organization Akatsuki begins hunting tailed beasts. Naruto faces members of this dangerous group while searching for Sasuke and protecting his village.

Crisis

Pain's Assault

Pain attacks the Hidden Leaf Village, destroying it completely. Naruto returns to face Pain in an epic battle that tests his resolve and ninja way.

War

Fourth Great Ninja War

The ninja world unites against the threat of the Ten-Tails and Madara Uchiha. Naruto plays a crucial role in the war, finally gaining the respect he always sought.

Achievement

Seventh Hokage

Naruto's dream comes true as he becomes the Seventh Hokage of the Hidden Leaf Village. He now protects the village and works toward a peaceful ninja world.

Naruto Universe

A tribute to Masashi Kishimoto's masterpiece and the ninja way that inspires millions.
